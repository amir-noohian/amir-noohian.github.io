Amir Noohian

I am a robotics researcher at the intersection of control and machine learning, specializing in teleoperation, rigid-body dynamics, and learning-enabled control for robotic arms. My work integrates model-based methods with data-driven approaches to enhance transparency, stability, and adaptability in human–robot interaction. I have strong skills in C++, Python, and ROS, along with extensive hands-on experience implementing and testing control algorithms on real robotic systems.
